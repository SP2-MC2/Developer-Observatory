Developer Observatory

Welcome to Developer Observatory, a browser-based virtual laboratory platform that enables controlled programming experiments while retaining most of the observational power of lab studies. The Developer Observatory can be used to conduct large-scale, reliable online programming studies with reasonable external validity.
If you are interested in the backgrounds of the tool, you'll be able to find the paper here after the CSET'17 workshop.
